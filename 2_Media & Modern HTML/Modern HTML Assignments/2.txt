2. What are HTML Entities. List out 5 commonly used HTML entities.

Ans: HTML entities are special codes or sequences of characters used to represent reserved characters, special symbols, or characters with a specific meaning in HTML.

Here are five commonly used HTML entities:

1. &amp; - Exclamation mark - &#33;
2. &amp; - Ampersand - &#38;
3. &lt; - Less than sign - &#60;
4. &gt; - Greater than sign - &#62;
5. &dollar; - Dollar sign - &#36;
